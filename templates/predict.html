Prediction Methodology

This system uses a Logistic Regression machine learning model trained on 300 customer records to predict churn probability.

Factor Impact on Churn Reasoning
Monthly Charges High → Higher Risk Higher costs increase financial burden and reduce perceived value.
Monthly Contract Higher Risk Short-term contracts make it easy for customers to leave anytime.
Yearly Contract Lower Risk Long-term commitment indicates trust and loyalty.
Age Younger → Higher Risk Younger customers tend to switch services more frequently.

Model: Logistic Regression — Accuracy: 78.3% on test data.
