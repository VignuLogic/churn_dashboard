How Prediction Works

This system checks a few basic customer details to guess whether the customer may leave (churn) or stay.

Factor What it Means Effect
Monthly Charges How much the customer pays every month Higher cost → More chance to leave
Contract Type Monthly or Yearly plan Monthly → More chance to leave
Yearly Plan Long-term plan Less chance to leave
Final Result Combines all factors System shows Stay or Churn

👉 Right now, this is a simple rule-based system. 👉 Later, we can improve it using Machine Learning for better accuracy.
